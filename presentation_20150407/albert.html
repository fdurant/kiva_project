Albert

Kisii, Kenya Agriculture | Dairy
  • Loan Overview
  • Repayment Schedule
  • Lender Comments
Albert is 37 years old, married to Rudiah and blessed with five children. He has been operating as a fruits and vegetable seller for the last four years and earns KES 8,000 per month.

Albert has applied for his first loan of KES 25,000 from VisionFund Kenya to buy a dairy cow to increase his income from the supply of milk. He plans to use the anticipated profits to educate his children and to expand his supplies in the market.

His future hope and dream is to be financially stable.

Additional Information

About VisionFund Kenya

VisionFund Kenya is dedicated to economically empowering its clients by providing financial services to improve living conditions of micro-entrepreneurs and their children across Kenya. VisionFund Kenya is the microfinance subsidiary of World Vision Kenya. Obtain more information on VisionFund Kenya from its website.
